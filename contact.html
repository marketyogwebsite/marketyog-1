How to reach us

Whether you’re a candidate looking for the next role, a hiring manager needing a fast shortlist, or a training partner who wants to collaborate — we make it simple to connect. Use any of the channels below and include a short note about what you need so we can route your request to the right person.

Email

For role submissions, candidate follow-ups, or partnership enquiries: officiallyteam@marketyog.com. Use subject lines like "Hiring: Sales Executives — Mumbai" or "Candidate Application — [Your Name]" so we can prioritise correctly.

Walk-in & Regional Offices

We work across multiple cities and coordinate interviews remotely as required. If you prefer an in-person meeting, mention your city in the email and we’ll propose the nearest regional partner or co-working address.

Tell us about your hiring need (sample brief)

To get a quick response, include the following in your message:

  • Role title & level: e.g., Sales Executive (Field) / Customer Care — 0–2 years
  • Location & shift: City, remote, or hybrid; day/evening shifts if relevant
  • Target metrics: Calls per day, conversion expectation, SLA for responses
  • Interview process: Number of rounds, on-site or virtual, assessment tasks
  • Start date & hiring timeline: Immediate, within 2 weeks, or next month

For candidates — apply with confidence

If you are applying for a role, please share an updated resume, two lines about your recent achievement, and the preferred job IDs (if listed on the site). We provide short, helpful feedback to applicants and guide them through interview readiness where possible.
