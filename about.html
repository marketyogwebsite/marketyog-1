About MarketYog

Your trusted partner for hiring trained Sales & Customer Care professionals — fast, fair, and future-ready.

Who we are

MarketYog began with a simple mission: bridge the gap between businesses that need reliable customer-facing talent and candidates who are eager, trained, and ready to contribute from day one. We are a boutique placement partner focused on Sales and Customer Care — two functions that directly shape a company's reputation and revenue. Over the years we've refined a practical, human-first approach that combines careful screening, soft-skill evaluation and role-matching to deliver consistently strong shortlists.

What sets us apart

  • Contextual screening: We assess candidates for the specific sales or support context (inbound vs outbound, B2B vs B2C) rather than using generic checklists.
  • First-interview ready: We conduct the initial screening and soft-skill interview so your hiring team spends time only with high-probability candidates.
  • Speed + quality: Shortlisting timelines are measured in days, not weeks — with a quality gate that keeps retention and performance strong.
  • Transparent process: We share clear candidate notes, recordings (with permission), and an objective scorecard for every submission.

How we work — a simple 3-step approach

  1. Understand your role:

    We begin with a short scoping call to learn about your product, KPIs, ideal candidate profile and interview process. This context ensures we screen for culture-fit as well as skill-fit.

  2. Source & screen:

    Our team uses a mix of targeted outreach, referral networks and training partner lists. Each candidate goes through a structured screening — verifying experience, attitude, situational responses and role-specific competency.

  3. Shortlist & support:

    We send a compact shortlist with candidate summaries and practical notes. If you choose to interview, we’ll help coordinate schedules, share interview templates, and follow up to close faster.

For candidates: why apply via MarketYog?

Applying through MarketYog is more than submitting a CV — it is partnering with a team that prepares you for interviews, highlights your strengths to hiring managers, and helps you land roles that match your skillset and goals.

  • Pre-screened roles: We only present roles that match your experience and growth path.
  • Interview help: Candidates get prep tips and a clear brief before they meet hiring companies.
  • Faster feedback: We prioritize timely updates — so you're never left guessing.

For hiring teams: the MarketYog advantage

Whether you are a startup scaling quickly or an established company building a resilient support team, our processes are tuned to reduce hiring friction and improve first-month productivity.

  • Curated candidates: We deliver smaller, higher-quality shortlists so interview cycles are efficient.
  • Role coaching: We recommend onboarding suggestions and a realistic probation checklist to increase new-hire success.
  • Flexible engagement: Hire direct, or use us for rapid contract-to-hire placements.

Our promise

We promise clear communication, respect for candidate privacy, and an honest partnership. If a hire doesn’t meet expectations in the initial period, we stay engaged to understand the gaps and work with you to make it right.
